Two E Bar & Lounge

A Neo-Classical lounge serving traditional Afternoon Tea by day, Two E Bar transforms into a hidden Art Deco gem by night. Originally the Gentlemen’s Library at The Pierre, Two E was redesigned by Alexandra Champalimaud to reflect an effortlessly chic Upper East Side residence.

The Pierre

Forbes Travel Guide
Five-Star Rating

Since 2013, The Pierre has been recognized with the prestigious Forbes Travel Guide Five-Star Rating, which has been the gold standard in hospitality for nearly 60 years. The distinction is awarded annually to fewer than 60 properties throughout the United States. A team of expert inspectors anonymously evaluate properties against up to 800 rigorous and objective standards. These standards are the most stringent in the industry — Forbes inspectors travel incognito, staying two nights and three days to gather every detail of the visit.

The Pierre is the only Forbes Five-Star and AAA Five Diamond property on Manhattan's Upper East Side.

The Pierre

EarthCheck Gold Certified

The Pierre is proud to be an EarthCheck Gold Certified hotel. EarthCheck is the world's leading, most rigorous and scientifically accurate program for the tourism industry. Hotels achieve Gold Certification after five years of continuous benchmarking. Since 1987, EarthCheck has helped benchmark environmental, social, energy, legal and community performance, as well as the design aspects of hotel buildings and their operations against peers in their sector, region and climate zone. Specific global benchmarking standards include sustainable development policy, water and energy utilization, waste management, paper usage, chemical usage, and corporate social responsibility initiatives.
